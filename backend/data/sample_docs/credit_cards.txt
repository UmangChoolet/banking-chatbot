CREDIT CARD GUIDE - TERMS, BENEFITS & POLICIES
================================================

TYPES OF CREDIT CARDS OFFERED

1. CLASSIC CARD (Entry Level)
Annual Fee: INR 500 (waived on spending INR 1 lakh/year)
Credit Limit: INR 50,000 to INR 2 lakhs
Reward Points: 1 point per INR 100 spent
Key Benefits: Fuel surcharge waiver, EMI conversion facility

2. GOLD CARD (Mid-range)
Annual Fee: INR 1,000 (waived on spending INR 2 lakhs/year)
Credit Limit: INR 2 lakhs to INR 5 lakhs
Reward Points: 2 points per INR 100 spent
Key Benefits: Airport lounge access (2 visits/quarter), Cashback on dining (5%), 1% cashback on all spends

3. PLATINUM CARD (Premium)
Annual Fee: INR 2,500 (waived on spending INR 3 lakhs/year)
Credit Limit: INR 5 lakhs to INR 10 lakhs
Reward Points: 3 points per INR 100 spent
Key Benefits: Unlimited airport lounge access, Travel insurance, Dining discounts up to 20%, Concierge service, Golf privileges

4. SUPER PREMIUM / INFINITE CARD
Annual Fee: INR 10,000
Credit Limit: INR 10 lakhs and above (by invitation)
Reward Points: 5 points per INR 100 spent
Key Benefits: Global lounge access, Travel credits, Hotel upgrades, Dedicated relationship manager

CREDIT CARD INTEREST RATES (APR)
- Standard purchase APR: 3.5% per month (42% per annum)
- Cash advance APR: 3.75% per month (45% per annum)
- Interest-free period: Up to 50 days (if previous balance is paid in full)
- Minimum amount due: 5% of outstanding balance or INR 200, whichever is higher

FEES AND CHARGES
- Late payment fee: INR 100 to INR 1,300 (based on outstanding amount)
  * Up to INR 500: INR 100
  * INR 501 to INR 5,000: INR 500
  * INR 5,001 to INR 10,000: INR 750
  * Above INR 10,000: INR 1,300
- Cash advance fee: 2.5% of amount (min INR 500)
- Over-limit fee: INR 500 or 2.5% of over-limit amount
- Foreign transaction fee: 3.5% of transaction amount
- Duplicate statement fee: INR 100 per statement
- Card replacement fee: INR 200 (lost/damaged)

REWARD POINTS PROGRAM
- 1 reward point = INR 0.25 in value
- Points can be redeemed for: Flights, Hotels, Shopping vouchers, Cashback, Pay credit card bill
- Points expiry: 2 years from date of earning
- Bonus points: 5x points on birthday month, 3x on partner merchants

HOW TO APPLY FOR A CREDIT CARD
Eligibility:
- Age: 18 years and above (primary holder)
- Minimum income: INR 20,000/month (salaried); INR 2.5 lakh/year (self-employed)
- Good credit score preferred (700+)
- Existing bank account holder gets faster approval

Documents needed:
- Identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport)
- Address proof
- Income proof (latest salary slip or ITR)
- PAN Card (mandatory)

BILLING CYCLE AND PAYMENT
- Statement generation date: Fixed date every month (e.g., 5th of every month)
- Payment due date: 20 days after statement date
- Payment methods: Online banking, mobile app, NEFT, cheque, auto-debit

CREDIT CARD SECURITY FEATURES
- EMV chip technology
- Contactless payments (tap and pay)
- OTP for online transactions
- Virtual card number for online shopping
- Transaction alerts via SMS and email
- Temporary card block via app
- International transaction control via app

CREDIT LIMIT MANAGEMENT
- Credit limit increase: After 6 months of good usage history
- Temporary limit increase: Available for major purchases (approval required)
- Emergency credit limit: Up to 20% of existing limit for genuine emergencies
- You can set your own spending limits by category using the mobile app

EMI CONVERSION FACILITY
- Convert transactions above INR 3,000 into EMIs
- Tenures: 3, 6, 9, 12, 18, 24 months
- Processing fee: 1% or INR 199 (whichever is higher)
- Foreclosure charge: 3% of outstanding principal

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What happens if I miss a credit card payment?
A: Missing a payment results in a late fee, interest charges on the outstanding amount at 3.5% per month, and a negative impact on your CIBIL credit score. Persistent defaults can lead to card suspension.

Q: How do I dispute a transaction?
A: You can dispute a transaction through our mobile app, internet banking, or by calling customer care within 30 days of the transaction date. We investigate and resolve within 21 working days.

Q: Can I use my credit card internationally?
A: Yes, cards with international usage enabled can be used globally. Enable international usage from the mobile app before travelling. Foreign transaction fee of 3.5% applies.

Q: What is the minimum age to get an add-on card?
A: Add-on cardholders must be at least 18 years old. The primary cardholder is responsible for all add-on card transactions.
