SAVINGS ACCOUNTS

What is a Savings Account?
A savings account is a basic deposit account where you can safely store money and earn interest on your balance. Our savings accounts offer:
- Interest rate: 2.70% to 3.50% per annum (calculated daily, credited quarterly)
- Minimum balance: INR 1,000 (regular), INR 10,000 (premium)
- Free transactions: 5 ATM withdrawals/month (own ATMs unlimited)
- Debit card: Free Rupay/Visa/Mastercard debit card
- Internet banking and mobile app: Free access

Zero Balance Account (Basic Savings Deposit Account - BSDA):
- No minimum balance requirement
- Basic banking services included
- Available to all Indian citizens

HOME LOANS

Eligibility:
- Age: 21 to 70 years
- Minimum income: INR 30,000/month (salaried); INR 4 lakh/year (self-employed)
- Loan amount: INR 10 lakh to INR 10 crore
- LTV (Loan to Value): Up to 80% of property value

Interest Rates:
- Floating rate: Starting 8.40% p.a. (linked to RBI repo rate)
- Fixed rate (2-year lock): Starting 9.00% p.a.
- Women borrowers: Additional 0.05% discount

Home Loan Processing:
- Processing fee: 0.5% of loan amount (max INR 15,000)
- Technical/Legal charges: INR 5,000 to INR 15,000
- MOD (Memorandum of Deposit): As per state stamp duty
- Insurance: Recommended (HDFC Ergo/LIC HFL products available)

DIGITAL BANKING SERVICES

UPI (Unified Payments Interface):
- Instant money transfers 24/7
- Daily limit: INR 1 lakh (standard), INR 2 lakh (verified)
- Supports: PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM, our bank's app

NEFT/RTGS/IMPS:
- NEFT: Batch processing, free for online transactions, nominal branch fee
- RTGS: For transactions above INR 2 lakh, same day settlement
- IMPS: Instant 24/7 transfer, up to INR 5 lakh per transaction
- Charges: Free for amounts up to INR 1,000; INR 5 to INR 50 above that

Mobile Banking App Features:

- Bill payments (electricity, water, gas, mobile recharge)

- Cheque services (stop payment, status)

- Biometric login support

ACCOUNT SECURITY

How to keep your account safe:
1. Never share your PIN, password, or OTP with anyone (including bank staff)
2. Register for SMS alerts for all transactions
3. Regularly change your internet banking password
4. Use virtual keyboard for online banking
5. Report lost/stolen debit or credit cards immediately: 1800-XXX-XXXX
6. Enable two-factor authentication on mobile app

Fraud Reporting:
- Call our 24/7 fraud hotline: 1800-XXX-4567
- Email: fraud@ourbank.com
- Zero liability for reported unauthorized transactions (subject to timely reporting)

KYC (Know Your Customer) POLICY
- KYC is mandatory for all banking products per RBI guidelines
- Documents: Aadhaar (OVD), PAN, one address proof
- Re-KYC: Every 2 years for high-risk customers, 8-10 years for others
- Video KYC available for remote onboarding

GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
1. Level 1: Branch/Contact Center (resolve within 5 working days)
2. Level 2: Nodal Officer (escalate if unresolved)
3. Level 3: Banking Ombudsman (RBI) - for unresolved complaints after 30 days
Ombudsman portal: https://cms.rbi.org.in

IMPORTANT RBI REGULATIONS
- Deposit insurance: DICGC covers deposits up to INR 5 lakh per customer per bank
- Nomination: Strongly recommended for all accounts and deposits
- TDS on FD interest: 10% if annual interest exceeds INR 40,000 (INR 50,000 for seniors)
- Form 15G/15H: Submit to avoid TDS if total income below taxable limit
